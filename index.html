The Story

A young knight discovers an ancient guardian hidden deep beneath forgotten ruins. The guardian gifts him a magical gun capable of channeling powerful energy.

Armed with both sword and magic, the knight travels through four worlds, battling monsters, collecting legendary artifacts, and uncovering the secrets of a growing darkness.

His final challenge awaits in the Dark Citadel, where the Dark Lord prepares to consume the world.
